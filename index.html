november, 2025. mixed media, collage, video art, digital painting.
An exploration of purpose. Communicating how much time and effort goes into the sense of meaningless. How little work it takes to have an idea. Confronting the concept of difficulty as a construct and proposing time as the only truth in this case. Avi Franklin-Casseres collaborating in sound.
Using photos of facades of people and places, I create collages of scenery and characters. I piece together my scenes manually to express a range of thoughts and feelings. I use AI to animate parts, and compose everything manually. I decipher my feelings of the video into writings which explore world philosophy. Each video is an exercise of learning for me. I find comfort in communicating a larger idea into a visual form meaningful to anyone but myself. Leaving ‘thought’ to exist through this digital form of mockery and imagination, based on the unknown of our world. As an artist I am uncomfortable in knowingness. My videos explore fiction, but only through looking for ‘truth.’ It is the impossibility of finding truth that stretches my work further and further from a human perspective. I feel my humanity tested and at every step of being so close, and working so closely with these details of life, I can confront my perception and am left with nothing but questions.
